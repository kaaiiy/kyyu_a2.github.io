One of My Enjoyments

Hatsune Miku Colorful Stage (also known as Project Sekai) is one of my favorite apps to play on my free time. I started playing the game when it was first released in Japan and I wanted to wait for the global version (recently came out in December 2021) to come out. It is an anime music rhythm gacha game. Why do I enjoy it so much? It's because it is the first game that made me get out of my comfort zone and meet so many nice people. I was able to make so many friends by joining the community (thanks to meeting a top tierer, a person who has lots of experience in the competitive side of the game, on Twitch). I was also able to learn a lot more about the game in depth and attempted to aim for a specific rank which was first place for my favorite event, "You Deserve It! Break Time!". With the support of my friends, I was able to achieve it in June! It was really difficult because it happened during when summer school started for me, but it was a really fun experience.
